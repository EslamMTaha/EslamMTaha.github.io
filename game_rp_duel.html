RP Duel

A radiation-protection card duel. Reduce your opponent's dose-limit health to 0 before they drain yours.

  • Sources (α, β, γ) go on your Offensive line. Each of your turns they fire down their column at the opponent until they decay (half-life timer).
  • Shields go on your two Defensive lines and protect you from the opponent's sources. The Front line faces the enemy.
  • One card per turn.
  • Specialists: the RSO (on a shield line) boosts adjacent shields; the Reactor (on your offensive line) gives a bonus card every 2 turns — but it costs you an attack slot.
  • Inverse-Square (Distance): halves the next volley you take — the time-distance-shielding trio.
  • Hazards: Sabotage makes an enemy source leak (hurts them, nicks you); Criticality hits an enemy Reactor and starts a meltdown countdown; Unsealed Source contaminates a tile. Answer them with Waste Disposal, Decontamination, and Emergency Plan.
  • Utility: Crack degrades an enemy shield (High-Z → Low-Z, Low-Z → destroyed); Survey Meter reveals an enemy hand card; PPE shields one of your units from contamination (and keeps a contaminated RSO working).
α Alpha — stopped completely by any single shield.
β Beta — needs Low-Z (front) + High-Z (back). Low-Z alone helps a lot; High-Z alone barely (it makes bremsstrahlung!).
γ Gamma — needs High-Z + High-Z. Dense material only; Low-Z is nearly useless.

Victory!
