How to play:
Predict how the field bends each particle, then move the detector plate (right) to catch it.
α = positive & heavy → bends a little.
β = negative & light → bends a lot, opposite way.
γ = neutral → goes straight.
Regions vary: E/B field, vacuum (all straight), or matter (α absorbed, β scatters, γ penetrates).
After your first launch, particles keep coming automatically — faster over time.
3 misses = game over.

Field Deflector

A source fires α, β, or γ radiation through an electric field, a magnetic field, empty vacuum, or a slab of matter. Each region affects the three radiations differently. Read the region, predict the path, and move your detector plate to catch the particle!

α — charge +2, heavy → small deflection
β− — charge −1, light → large deflection (opposite to α)
γ — neutral → no deflection, travels straight
