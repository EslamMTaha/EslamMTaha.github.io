Rad Defense

Radiation tower-defense. Enemies march in from the right — stop them before they cross the left edge. Build generators for energy and radiation sources to attack, matching the radiation to the threat.

  • α Alpha — slow, short range, massive single hit. Useless vs shielded armor.
  • β Beta — moderate rate & damage, full lane.
  • γ Gamma — fast, low damage, pierces the whole lane — and the only thing that hurts Lead-Lined heavies.
  • n Neutron — ignores armor; a neutron kill on a ☢ fissile enemy triggers fission — 3 neutrons fly off at random angles and can chain-react across the field.
  • SMR / Fusion generate energy; CT Scan fires an X-ray fan; Trench slows ground enemies; Security guards a 3×3 from thieves; Nuke wipes a 2×2 (those tiles lock).
  • Activity×2 — click onto a source to double its fire rate. e⁻ Cloud & Brems. are lane tiles: place them in front of a Beta source — β beams passing through the cloud start to wander across lanes, and through the bremsstrahlung target turn into piercing X-rays.

Threats: 🦠 swarms, the 📡 scout (heads for your weakest lane), 🦅 thieves (fly in to steal a tower — block with Security), ⊟ control rods (absorb neutrons, stopping fission chains), and rare Rad-Mutants (any hit can mutate an enemy). Clear the assault to win; a breach of the left edge costs a life.
