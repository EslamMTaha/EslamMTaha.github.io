How to play:
Dose obeys the inverse-square law: each source adds strength ÷ distance².
Move the boxes so the worker's total dose is as low as possible. Put the strongest sources in the farthest cells.

Dose Minimizer

Move the radioactive source boxes around the worker to minimise the dose they receive — using the inverse-square law. Strongest source belongs farthest away. Reach the lowest possible dose to clear each of the 6 levels.
